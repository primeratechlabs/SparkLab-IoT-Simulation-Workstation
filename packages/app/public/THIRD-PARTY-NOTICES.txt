A. In-browser toolchains served under /toolchain, /esp32-classic-toolchain
==============================================================================

1) GNU GCC - the C/C++ compiler (cc1, cc1plus), compiled to WebAssembly.
   Used for: Arduino Uno (AVR) client-side compile.
   Version: GCC 14.2.0  (GCC_VERSION, pinned in ci/toolchain-builder/Dockerfile)
   License: GPL-3.0-or-later.  https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.html
   Runtime libraries (libgcc, libstdc++): GPL-3.0-or-later WITH
   GCC-Runtime-Library-Exception-3.1 (the exception lets compiled programs link
   them without becoming GPL).

2) GNU Binutils - the assembler + linker (avr-as, avr-ld, avr-objcopy),
   compiled to WebAssembly.
   Version: 2.43  (BINUTILS_VERSION, pinned in ci/toolchain-builder/Dockerfile)
   License: GPL-3.0-or-later.

3) LLVM / Clang / LLD - the C/C++ compiler + linker for the ESP32 targets
   (clang.mjs, lld.mjs), compiled to WebAssembly. Includes the Espressif Xtensa
   backend.
   Version: esp-clang (LLVM, esp toolchain build).
   License: Apache-2.0 WITH LLVM-exception.  https://llvm.org/LICENSE.txt
   (Permissive - attribution only; no source offer required.)
